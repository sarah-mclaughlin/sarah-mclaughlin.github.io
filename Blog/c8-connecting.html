Complete the political awareness exercise here in writing.

I live in a flat of five other people. Our fridge sometimes becomes very full with food bits that slowly decay and become mouldy as noone thinks the food is theirs. Sometimes I go through the fridge and throw out food that has gone off or scraps that don't look like are going to be eaten. My flatmate once became upset with me because I threw out his one piece of white bread that he had left in the fridge. I understand that, from his perspective, he did not like his food being thrown out. It was a simple mistake and we have since moved past this.
