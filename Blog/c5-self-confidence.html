What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self awareness helps us to percieve our emotions with more clarity allowing us to be more mindful and vice versa.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I found the body scan exercise useful. I found it interesting how it relaxed my body and almost helped me pinpoint areas where I might be 'carrying stress'. I like that the body scan uses awareness of the body to create awareness of the mind and expands on the idea that the mind and body are linked.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I think I have been going through my day-to-day knowing that my emotions are what I am feeling, not who I am, and that, if I apply this thinking, I gain control over my emotions. I could however always be quicker to apply this thinking in situations that challenge me emotionally.
