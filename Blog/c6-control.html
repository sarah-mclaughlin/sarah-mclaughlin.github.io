When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

I find in programming, if I get stuck or blocked and can't work it out I can become overwhelmed and frustrated. It is during these times that I might experience shallow breathing or a faster heart-rate. These moments are a good opportunity for me to practice Siberian North Railroad exercise.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I found it an interesting exercise. I think it is difficult to evaluate its effetciveness because I did it in a relatively relaxed context. The real test will be implementing this technique when I have been 'triggered'. I have no doubt that the SBNRR approach will have a positive impact in relation to how I deal with and react to my emotions when 'triggered'.
