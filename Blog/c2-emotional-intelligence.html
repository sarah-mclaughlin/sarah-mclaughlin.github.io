What learnings did you take from the chapter

Reading the chapter 'Even an engineer can thrive on emotional intelligence' taught me that there are five domains of emotional intellugence:

  • self-awareness
  • self-regulation
  • motivation
  • empathy; and
  • social skills
Emotional intelligence is an important quality in a wide range of work spaces and industries. It the core of what allows individuals to work and succeed as a team. Leaders with a high emotional intelligence are better at cultivating open and honest lines of communication within a team/organisation. What is particularly interesting is that emotional intelligence can be learned. This links to the concept of neuroplasticity and the idea that what we think, do, and pay attention to, changes the structure and function of our brains. This means that we can intentionally grow and develop our emotional intelligence through training
